Why do we dream?

Scientists still don’t fully understand dreams. Some believe they help process emotions, while others think they’re just random brain activity.

Why do we get hiccups?

Hiccups are caused by sudden contractions of the diaphragm, but the exact reason isn’t always clear.

Why is the sky blue?

Because blue light scatters more in the atmosphere than other colors.
